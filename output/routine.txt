Wilson's Red-Headed League routine: He was hired to work in an office copying text (notably encyclopedia entries) by hand for several hours each day. This repetitive copying was a distraction so criminals could tunnel into a nearby bank. Eventually, the League suddenly dissolved, revealing the job was a fraud.
